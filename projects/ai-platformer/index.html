AI Platformer

Welcome to the AI Platformer! In this game, you get to design a level, and then watch as a bunch of little AI agents try to navigate through it. You can see how they learn and adapt over time, by watching how thier brains evolve and change as they play the game. It's a fun an interesting way to see how AI can learn and improve, and at the end, you can watch each individual species to see how well they did!
